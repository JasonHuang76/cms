Chuan Tien Fishery Machine Co.,Ltd.was established by Mr. Chang-Yan Lin in 1984. As a pioneer in manufacturing saltwater pump, we have made lot efforts to keep inventing products that fit our customers' needs, and promote sea-farming machinery in Taiwan .

Since saltwater pump is the major equipment of sea-farming, developing the pump that especially fits salt water could increase profits in aquaculture. Therefore, Chuan-Tian aquatic machinery corp. keeps inventing new designs, and is the first to use VFA and VTSIC materials in our products.
